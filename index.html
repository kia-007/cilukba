About Us

Cilukbaa Preschool & Kindergarten, and Sekolah Bina Tunas Cemerlang are two integrated schools in Bogor.

Cilukbaa Preschool & Kindergarten is established in 2004. Our school has four levels in guiding children within their golden age years. Starts from the early year, Toddler, Preschool, Kindergarten A and Kindergarten B.

Sekolah Bina Tunas Cemerlang is for the Primary levels. It is established to support the continuation of providing a good education in primary leve

Our Mission

Our school believes that giving a good, structured, complete and heart to heart education is the best foundation in educating the students. Aside from structural lessons, we also share what we know about manner, character building and soft skills that will give tremendous outcomes and bring the best of the students.

Students learn and practice values and good attitudes in daily learning at school. Our mission is to prepare them to be bright, mindful and open-minded characters at school, home and in society

Our Program

We acknowledge that students learn from many things around them. Through books, teachers, peers, hands on activities, and first-hand observations. Therefore, in accordance to learning, we have theme learning, school trips, after school activities and monthly presentation as an addition to their education.
